About Matthew Cobbs

Matthew Cobbs is a 28 year-old web developer from Detroit. As both an in-house and freelance developer, he has several years of experience with planning, designing, developing, and maintaining websites.

Matthew possesses in-depth knowledge of common web languages--including PHP, Javascript, JQuery, Classic ASP, XHTML and SQL--as well as applications--including Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, IIS, Apache, MySQL, and SQL Server--and platforms--including Wordpress and Drupal.
